Anakin Skywalker

The man that gets me through life

Here's a timeline of Anakin's life

  • 41 BBY - Skywalker was born on the desert planet of Tatooine in the Outer Rim Territories. He was the son of Shmi Skywalker, a slave who conceived a child without a father. His blood contained over twenty-thousand midi-chlorians, surpassing that of Grand Master Yoda and all other Jedi in the galaxy.
  • 32 BBY - Following the Battle of Naboo, the Jedi High Council admitted Skywalker into the Order as the Padawan of Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi despite feeling that he was too old and emotional to adhere to the Jedi Code.
  • 22 BBY - The start of the Clone Wars. Anakin falls in love with Padame (which defies the Jedi-code) he actively particpates in the war and struggles with himself to remain Obi-Wan Kenobi's apprentice. He single-handedly duels a Sith Lord by the name of Count Doku and gets his hand sliced off in the process. He also finds out that his mother was murdered by Tusken Raiders at the end and retaliates by killing their entire tribe which introduces us to his Dark-side.
