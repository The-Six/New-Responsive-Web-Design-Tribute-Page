Anakin Skywalker

The man who gets me through life

Here's a timeline of Anakin's life

  • 41 BBY - Skywalker was born on the desert planet of Tatooine in the Outer Rim Territories. He was the son of Shmi Skywalker, a slave who conceived a child without a father. His blood contained over twenty-thousand midi-chlorians, surpassing that of Grand Master Yoda and all other Jedi in the galaxy.
  • 32 BBY - Following the Battle of Naboo, the Jedi High Council admitted Skywalker into the Order as the Padawan of Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi despite feeling that he was too old and emotional to adhere to the Jedi Code.
  • 22 BBY - The start of the Clone Wars. Anakin falls in love with Padame (which defies the Jedi-code) he actively particpates in the war and struggles with himself to remain Obi-Wan Kenobi's apprentice. He single-handedly duels a Sith Lord by the name of Count Doku and gets his hand sliced off in the process. He also finds out that his mother was murdered by Tusken Raiders at the end and retaliates by killing their entire tribe which introduces us to his Dark-side.
  • 19 BBY - Skywalker's faith in the Jedi High Council begins to shake when Tano left the Jedi Order in 19 BBY, Skywalker learns that Supreme Chancellor Sheev Palpatine is Darth Sidious, the elusive Dark Lord of the Sith who orchestrated the Clone war in order to take over the galaxy. Manipulated into believing that the Jedi had failed him and coupled with his fear of losing Amidala in childbirth, Skywalker betrays and turns against the Jedi Order, believing Sidious had the knowledge to cheat death. Consumed by the persona of Darth Vader, the fallen Jedi Knight marches his legion on the Jedi Temple of Coruscant where they executed its inhabitants, including Jedi younglings, as part of Order 66 and the Great Jedi Purge. Vader was then sent to Mustafar to exterminate the Separatist Council at the behest of his Sith Master, who became the self-proclaimed Galactic Emperor. Vader subsequently sustained severe injuries in his duel with Kenobi, and was rebuilt as an armored cyborg while his wife died of a broken heart.
